## Usage Guidance

- use cream and soft neutrals for breathing room
- use moss and walnut for structure
- use honey and lantern glow for warmth and calls to action
- use blue and blush accents sparingly to keep the palette lively

Avoid a return to pure monochrome dominance except for small moments of contrast.
